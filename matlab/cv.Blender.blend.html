cv.Blender/blend

Blends and returns the final pano

[dst, dst_mask] = obj.blend()
[dst, dst_mask] = obj.blend('OptionName',optionValue, ...)

Output

  • dst Final pano, of type int16.
  • dst_mask Final pano mask, of type uint8.

Options

  • Dst
  • Mask
