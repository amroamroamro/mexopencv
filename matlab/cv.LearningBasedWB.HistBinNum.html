cv.LearningBasedWB/HistBinNum

Defines the size of one dimension of a three-dimensional RGB histogram that is used internally by the algorithm. It often makes sense to increase the number of bins for images with higher bit depth (e.g. 256 bins for a 12 bit image). default 64
