cv.Utils.setUseIPP_NE

Enable/disable use of IPP_NE

cv.Utils.setUseIPP_NE(tf)

Input

  • tf flag

IPP Not-Exact mode. This function may force use of IPP then both IPP and OpenCV provide proper results but have internal accuracy differences which have to much direct or indirect impact on accuracy tests.
