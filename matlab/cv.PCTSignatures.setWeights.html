cv.PCTSignatures/setWeights

Sets weights (multiplicative constants) that linearly stretch individual axes of the feature space

obj.setWeights(weights)

Input

  • weights Values of all weights. A float vector of the form [weight, X, Y, L, A, B, Contrast, Entropy].
