cv.SimpleWB

Simple white balance algorithm

A simple white balance algorithm that works by independently stretching each of the input image channels to the specified range. For increased robustness it ignores the top and bottom P percent of pixel values.

See also
cv.SimpleWB/balanceWhite, cv.GrayworldWB, cv.LearningBasedWB, cv.cvtColor, cv.equalizeHist, imadjust, stretchlim
