getMean (cv.BasicFaceRecognizer) - MATLAB File Help
cv.BasicFaceRecognizer/getMean

Get sample mean

m = obj.getMean()

Output

  • m The sample mean calculated from the training data. A double vector of length w*h (width and height of a face image).

Note: returns an empty mat if the model is not trained.
