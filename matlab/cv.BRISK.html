cv.BRISK

Class implementing the BRISK keypoint detector and descriptor extractor

As described in [LCS11].

References

[LCS11]:

Stefan Leutenegger, Margarita Chli, and Roland Yves Siegwart. "BRISK: Binary Robust Invariant Scalable Keypoints". In IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV) 2011, pages 2548-2555. IEEE, 2011.
